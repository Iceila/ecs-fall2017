Resources


THIS WEBSITE ISN’T ENOUGH.

I’ve only given you the bare minimum of knowledge for every fundamental. You need more than just an overview to become an art master.

The resources below are all art tutorial resources. I've found these three resources in particular to be extremely helpful in my art journey. Below you'll find the name and link to that resource, as well as a quick overview of what each resource teaches.





PROKO.COM

Despite having courses on mostly the human figure and anatomy, has become extremely popular. Although the main focus is on the human body, Proko.com also has tutorials that break the funamentals down nicely. Every course is broken down into bite-sized chunks so that anyone can learn how to draw. New courses are coming out all the time.










THE ART OF WEI

Like Proko, Art of Wei focuses on humans, mostly human portraits. However, his older videos go more in depth into many of the fundamentals, such as perspective. Wei takes you through the steps slowly, and in plain English. Wei uploads videos regularly.










VOLEN CK

From knowing nothing about art to pursuing mastery, Volen has experienced it all. If you feel frustrated about your artworks, watching Volen can boost your spirits. He also teaches you a system that you can apply to any skill you’re studying, and his journey through each of the five fundamentals on this list.
