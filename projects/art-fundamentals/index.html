WHY LEARN ART?

Art is beneficial to your health. Like a diary, you can find ways to express yourself and boost your creativity.

Most people think that learning art is hard. But in fact, it is a very methodical and systematic process. It can be something you do for just a couple minutes a day, or spend hours laboring on.

Whatever the reason, art can even boost your performance in your job. Art is abstract, and can be applied to so many situations.

Finally, art will give you skills such as creativity, perseverance, and so much more.

WHAT’S ON THIS WEBSITE?

This website will cover the five art fundamentals that will kick-start your art journey.

The five sections are broken down into two basic parts: an overview of the fundamental and how to study it.

At the end of each page, there is a challenge that you can complete. This challenge tests your understanding of everything above.

On the resources page, there are links to other art websites that cover the fundamentals much more in depth.

THINGS TO REMEMBER

If you get through this website, read every line of text, did every challenge, that doesn’t mean your done.

This website is designed to get you started in the arts. You have to study to refine your skills. Visit the resources page to learn more about the fundamentals. Study every day.

Art is a never-ending learning process. You’re constantly refining your own style and improving.

Just because you’ve read the entire website doesn’t mean you’re a master at art.
